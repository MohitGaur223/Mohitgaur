Calorie Cloud

"CalorieCloud" is a fitness management portal with two sections: user and admin. Users can register and log in to establish fitness goals such as weight gain or loss. They can input workouts and exercises, monitor their progress with visual graphs, and receive weekly nutrition recommendations for effective weight management. This project encompasses several additional functionalities. For its development, I employed Java and Spring Boot for the backend, along with MySQL. On the frontend, I utilized JavaScript, React.js, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to ensure a comprehensive and feature-rich experience.

Med Assist

MedAssist is a medical website designed to facilitate seamless interactions between patients and healthcare professionals. Patients can easily select a doctor based on their specific medical concerns. To assess the patient's medical condition accurately, we've implemented a comprehensive survey. After evaluating the patient's responses, our system enables doctors to provide precise prescriptions. If necessary, patients can further consult with their designated healthcare providers. The website was expertly crafted using a suite of tools and languages, including Django, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL.
